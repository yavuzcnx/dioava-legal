Chloris — Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 20, 2026 · Last updated: April 20, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Dioava ("we", "us", "Dioava") collects, uses, stores, and shares user data in the Chloris mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you acknowledge that you have read and accepted this policy.

1. Data Controller

  • Developer: Dioava (sole proprietorship)
  • Country: Turkey
  • Contact: dumendenhelp@gmail.com

2. Data We Collect

2.1. Account Information

  • Email address (for registration and login)
  • Username / display name
  • Profile picture (optional, if uploaded)

2.2. Game and Content Data

  • Plants in your garden, plant types, growth status
  • Photos of plants you scan
  • Game progress, statistics, trade history
  • Pro subscription status

2.3. Device and Usage Data

  • Device model, OS version, language preference
  • App version and identifiers

2.4. Location Data

To make the in-app seasonal system work correctly, we use your device's approximate location (at city/region level). We do not record or share precise GPS coordinates. You can grant or revoke location permission at any time from your device settings.

2.5. Camera and Photo Library

We request access to your camera and photo library for the plant scanning feature. This access is used only to process the photo you choose or capture.

2.6. Notifications

We send push notifications for plant care reminders and in-game events. You can disable notifications from your device settings at any time.

3. How We Use Your Data

  • To provide the App's core features (plant scanning, garden management, seasonal system, trading)
  • To manage and secure your account
  • To offer AI-assisted plant identification and care recommendations
  • To enable Pro subscription features
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and improve the App
  • To comply with our legal obligations

4. Third-Party Services

Our App uses the following third-party services to provide core functionality. Each has its own privacy policy, and your data is subject to that policy once transmitted to the respective service.

4.1. Supabase (Backend and Database)

We use Supabase for user accounts, game data, and file storage. Data is stored on Supabase's secure servers.
Policy: supabase.com/privacy

4.2. Anthropic (Claude AI)

We use Anthropic's Claude API for plant identification, care recommendations, and other AI features. Your photo and related data may be sent to Anthropic for processing.
Policy: anthropic.com/legal/privacy

4.3. OpenAI (GPT-4o)

We use OpenAI's GPT-4o model for plant image generation (healthy/wilting/dead variants) and certain AI features.
Policy: openai.com/policies/privacy-policy

4.4. Google AdMob (Advertising)

We use Google AdMob to serve ads within the App. AdMob may process device identifiers and usage data to personalize ads. This applies to users aged 13 and above.
Policy: policies.google.com/technologies/ads
You can manage ad personalization via Google Ad Settings.

4.5. Apple App Store / Google Play Billing (Subscriptions)

Pro subscription purchases are processed directly by Apple or Google. We never access or store your payment information. These transactions are governed by Apple's and Google's own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We only share your data in the following cases:

  • With the service providers listed above (only to the extent necessary for the service to function)
  • When required by law (court order, government request, etc.)
  • When necessary to protect user safety or the service

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as your account is active and as required by our legal obligations. When you delete your account, your personal data is removed from our systems within a reasonable time (no later than 30 days). Data required for legal compliance is retained only for the applicable period.

7. Your Rights

Under GDPR and Turkey's KVKK (Law No. 6698), you have the right to:

  • Access your data and request a copy
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Object to or restrict data processing
  • Receive your data in a portable format
  • Withdraw your consent at any time

To exercise these rights, please email dumendenhelp@gmail.com.

8. Account Deletion

To delete your account and all associated data, use the in-app option at "Settings > Delete Account" or email dumendenhelp@gmail.com. Requests are processed within 30 days.

9. Security

We apply industry-standard security measures (encrypted connections, secure authentication, access control) to protect your data. However, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

10. Children's Privacy

Chloris is intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it from our systems promptly. If you believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us.

11. International Data Transfer

Our service providers (Supabase, Anthropic, OpenAI, Google) may host your data in countries outside Turkey (such as the EU and US). All transfers are carried out under appropriate legal safeguards (Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions, etc.).

12. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. For material changes, we will notify you via the App or email. The current version is always published on this page, and the "last updated" date is revised accordingly.

13. Contact

For any questions, requests, or complaints regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices:

  • Email: dumendenhelp@gmail.com
  • Developer: Dioava, Turkey
